About DentalPost

Our Mission

At DentalPost, we are committed to helping dental professionals connect and create teams that excel. Our ecosystem is built to help educate and improve the lives of our community.

About DentalPost

We are a purpose-built company designed to help you improve lives through self-awareness of core values, personality traits, workplace culture and skills. This is the recipe to a more fulfilling career and life. Our unique online community matches dental professionals with dental offices to create teams that excel. DentalPost helps you make a better job choice or hiring decision.

Meet the Hygienist Behind
DentalPost

Tonya Lanthier, RDH Founder | CEO

Tonya Lanthier is the founder and CEO of DentalPost, the premier online and mobile dental job board and community. The dental tooth duchess entrepreneur is passionate about improving lives and helping dental professionals build teams that excel by using data to make smarter hires. She is an uber connector and optimist that loves adventure - a true MavHERick.

Tonya started DentalPost in 2005 as a tool to help dental professionals' connect and make better job choices and hiring decisions by using data. Since then, the company has grown into a networking community for more than 750,000 dental professionals and 38,000 dental offices. DentalPost has made the Inc. 5000 list of fastest growing private companies two years in a row.

Tonya is a member of the American Dental Hygienists' Association (ADHA), Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO), a supporter of AACD's Give Back a Smile program and a volunteer at several charitable organizations including Georgia Mission of Mercy and Ben Mansell Clinic.

Tonya Lanthier, RDH Founder | CEO

She is a board member of the Oral Cancer Cause and Dental Entrepreneur Women. Tonya speaks at popular dental conferences sharing her story and how to build winning teams. She also speaks to dental hygienist students, and coaches job seekers in their careers.

Tonya is a native of Atlanta and a mother of twin thirteen-year old girls.
